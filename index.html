AI-Powered Student Mental Wellness Platform

MindEase Health is a web-based support system designed to help students monitor their mood, access digital wellbeing resources, and receive personalized emotional support suggestions.

Launch Platform

Emotion Analysis

Users can describe how they feel in text form and receive an immediate mood classification with support advice.

Mood Tracking

The system stores user mood entries in a database to support emotional reflection and progress monitoring.

Support Resources

Students can access practical mental wellbeing recommendations, stress-reduction guidance, and self-care tips.

Session Booking

The platform includes a booking form for students to request guidance and support sessions in a structured way.
