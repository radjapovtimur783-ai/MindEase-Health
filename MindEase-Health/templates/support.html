Support Resources

Helpful self-care guidance for stress, anxiety, and emotional wellbeing

Stress Relief

Use short breaks, breathing exercises, and realistic study planning to reduce academic pressure.

Sleep Recovery

Protect your sleep routine, reduce late-night screen time, and avoid all-night studying where possible.

Talk to Someone

Reach out to trusted friends, family members, tutors, or university support staff instead of staying alone with stress.

Healthy Habits

Drink water, move regularly, and create a balanced daily routine with time for recovery and focus.
